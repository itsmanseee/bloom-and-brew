Our Story

A place that
feels like home

Born from a passion for perfectly crafted coffee and the beauty of botanicals, Bloom & Brew opened its doors in 2014 with a simple belief: every cup should tell a story.

We source our beans directly from small-batch farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — roasted fresh weekly to carry the full expression of their origin.

Beyond the coffee, we have curated a space filled with living plants, warm light, and the kind of unhurried atmosphere that invites you to stay a little longer.

Our Space

Designed for slow mornings

Every corner of Bloom & Brew has been thoughtfully designed to feel like a living garden — filled with natural light, trailing botanicals, and the gentle hum of conversation.

Whether you claim a window seat with a book, set up for a morning of work, or gather with friends over brunch, there is a corner here that is yours.

We seat 48 guests across our indoor garden room and sunlit back terrace, with private dining available for groups of up to 20.
